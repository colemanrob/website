Type Convert

Never noticed type_convert from readr that lets you wrangle data and then convert as appropriate. Let’s give it a spin. library(tidyverse) typical_data <- tribble(~bad_col, "3_apple", "4_banana") typical_data %>% separate(bad_col, into = c("n", "fruit"), sep = "_") %>% type_convert() ## # A tibble: 2 x 2 ## n fruit ## <dbl> <chr> ## 1 3 apple ## 2 4 banana Perfect!
